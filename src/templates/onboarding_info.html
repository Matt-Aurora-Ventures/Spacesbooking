Our X Spaces Engagement Process

Welcome to the Aurora Ventures X Spaces engagement portal! We\'re excited to collaborate with you. Hereâ€™s a general overview of our process from initial contact to post-Space promotion. Your dashboard will reflect the current status of your specific engagement.

Phase 1: Initial Contact & Qualification

  • Target Identification & Initial Data Entry: We identify potential guests and projects that align with our audience and themes.
  • Pre-Contact Research: We do our homework to understand your project and recent achievements to ensure a relevant and engaging conversation.
  • Initial Reach Out: We make first contact, typically via X or Telegram, with a personalized message.
  • Converse & Confirm Interest: We discuss the possibility of an X Space and confirm your interest and availability.

Phase 2: Preparation & Content Creation

  • Gather Assets for Canva Graphic: We\'ll work with you to get your logo, a brief summary of your project, your website, and X.com social links for the promotional graphic.
  • Graphic Creation: Our team designs a custom graphic for the X Space using your assets and our branding.
  • Founder Research (In-Depth): We conduct more detailed research to help formulate insightful questions.
  • Question Generation & Formatting: We draft a set of questions tailored to your project and our audience, referencing our style guide for consistency.
  • Submit Questions for Review: We send the drafted questions to you for review and approval to ensure accuracy and comfort.

Phase 3: Scheduling & Promotion

  • Set Date & Time: We finalize the date and time for the X Space with you.
  • Confirm Graphic with Founder: We share the promotional graphic with you for final approval.
  • Prepare Initial Promotional Tweet Content: We draft the initial announcement tweet.
  • Create X Space: We set up the official X Space on the platform.
  • Confirm with Founder (Final): We send a final confirmation with all details, including the X Space link.
  • Schedule in Calendar: We provide an .ics file for you to easily add the event to your calendar.
  • Schedule/Post Initial Promotional Tweet: The announcement tweet goes live!
  • One-Hour Pre-Space Reminder & Promotion: We\'ll send out a reminder tweet and post in relevant Telegram channels one hour before the Space goes live.

Phase 4: Post-Space Promotion & Follow-up (Updated)

  • Post-Space Promotion Tweets: After the Space, we share a tweet with a video/recording snippet and another tweet linking to the full recording.
  • Create Animated Sound Byte: An engaging animated sound byte is created from a key moment in the Space (typically 2 days later).
  • Retweet with Sound Byte: The animated sound byte is shared via a retweet of the original Space announcement or a new promotional tweet.
  • Write & Publish Substack Article: A recap article or a piece inspired by the Space discussion is written and published on Substack.
  • Submit All Promotional Material to Founder: We share all these promotional materials (tweets, sound byte, Substack link) with you for your own use and further promotion.
